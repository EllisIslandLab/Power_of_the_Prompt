Our story

From a beach cottage to your cup

Driftwood started in 2014 with a secondhand roaster on the Oregon coast and a stubborn belief that great coffee shouldn't sit on a shelf for months.

Today we roast for thousands of homes and a handful of cafés we love — but the rule hasn't changed: roast small, ship fast, taste everything.

— Mara & Eli, founders
